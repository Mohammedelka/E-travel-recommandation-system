Hotel Spa
The Manali Inn

The Manali Inn is a cosy hotel sitting across the banks of the mighty River Beas. Besides the 47 comfortable rooms, the hotel also has a fitness centre with sauna, steam and jacuzzi, spa rooms, a conference hall, a video game parlour and a discotheque. Manali Inn is located just three kilometres from the city centre, popularly known as Mall Road. Simsa Village Manali, Himachal Pradesh, 175131, India

Resort, Hotel, Luxury
ManuAllaya Resortspa, Manali

ManuAllaya is a luxury resort where you can either choose to book a spacious and elegantly designed room or opt for a private cottage. This is one of the handful of hotels in Manali which offer an indoor temperature controlled swimming pool. There are three restaurants at the resort Cafe Jardin, D’Jong and Kibber that serve western, Asian and Indian cuisines respectively. They also have a bar called Library, should you want to relax with a drink in the evening. Chadiyari Manali, Himachal Pradesh, 175131, India

Resort
The Himalayan, Hadimba Temple Road

As far as architecture is considered, there’s no other hotel in Manali which compares to the exquisite Victorian Gothic Revival style of The Himalayan. The luxury resort, set amid apple and cherry orchards, offers beautifully designed rooms all furnished with antique furniture and objets d’art. The Himalayan also works towards sustainable practices like energy and water conservation, waste reduction and local community outreach, making it ideal for those looking for an eco-friendly hotel in Manali
